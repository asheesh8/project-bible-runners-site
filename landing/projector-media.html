Group media

A projector turns one resource into a group moment.

Not every person will have a phone, storage, or confidence using files. Projector support lets a leader show gospel films, training clips, Scripture media, and children’s resources to a gathered group.

UsesSession flowPrepareGuide

What it supports

Group access when personal devices are limited.

01

Gospel films

Show media for evangelism, discipleship, children, or community events when a phone screen is too small.

02

Leader training

Use video or slide-based teaching for small groups, schools, or church leaders.

03

Scripture audio

Pair audio Scripture with a speaker so a group can listen together in a local language.

Session flow

Do a quick test before people gather.

Choose the place

Find shade or darkness, a stable table, and a surface that works as a screen.

Connect source

Use VillageServer, a phone, USB drive, or computer as the media source.

Test sound

People forgive imperfect video faster than inaudible sound. Test volume early.

Save next steps

After the session, help interested people save or receive the resource on their phones.

Prepare well

Most projector problems are setup problems.

  • Power planKnow whether the projector runs from battery, solar, or local power.
  • Input planKnow the cable or wireless adapter before the gathering starts.
  • Speaker planUse an external speaker if the group is larger than a room.
  • Backup fileKeep the same video on USB or phone if streaming fails.
