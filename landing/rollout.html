Rollout and Reach

Rollout should be measured honestly: where the kit went, who used it, what worked, what failed, and what changed because of it.

Plain answer

A board should see proof of concept before hearing large claims. Track pilots, training, resource use, and stories carefully.

Pilot

Start with a small number of trusted field partners and observe real use.

Train

Teach one clear user path: connect, open, save, share.

Measure

Count kits, locations, trained leaders, active users, downloads, and stories.

Improve

Use field feedback to simplify instructions and adjust the content library.
