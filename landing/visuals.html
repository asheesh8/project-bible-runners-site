Board brief - show the work simply

Photos and Visuals

Photos, diagrams, and short videos help non-technical viewers understand the field model quickly.

Plain answer

A good visual should answer one question at a glance: what is being used, who is using it, and why it matters.

Field steps

Use it this way

  1. Show the peopleUse photos of real setup, training, and handoff moments, not only equipment close-ups.
  2. Show the partsCapture the phone, server, battery, solar panel, projector, dish, and printed guide in context.
  3. Caption plainlyWrite captions that explain the ministry purpose in one sentence.
  4. Avoid clutterUse fewer, stronger images so the page feels like a story instead of a dump of files.
  5. Keep permissions clearOnly publish images and video that are approved for public ministry use.

Best photo

A person using the kit in a real setting.

Best diagram

A simple flow from power to server to phone to sharing.

Best video

A short setup clip that shows confidence and practicality.

Board takeaway

Visual proof makes the initiative easier to understand and easier to trust.
