Kit Levels and Costs

Kit planning should be understandable without a spreadsheet. Start with the ministry use case, then choose the right level of tools.

Plain answer

The right kit is not always the biggest kit. A phone-only distribution plan may be enough in one place; a solar, projector, and satellite kit may be needed somewhere else.

Basic Kit

Local library and simple phone access for small gatherings.

Media Kit

Adds projector and audio for group teaching.

Power Kit

Adds battery or solar support for weak-grid settings.

Satellite-Enabled Kit

Adds optional live connection for updates and support when justified.
