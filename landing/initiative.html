What we do

A portable library for places beyond reliable internet.

The VillageServer Initiative is a portable technology project designed to help bring Bible tools, gospel resources, and Christian media to unreached and underserved people groups around the world.

The model

Bring the library. Create local access.

A Raspberry Pi 5 stores Bible text, audio Scripture, gospel films, discipleship resources, children’s materials, training videos, and PDFs. It creates local Wi-Fi, so nearby devices browse without a data plan.

Language-specific microSD cards and USB drives make libraries easier to carry and swap. The system is designed to keep working when live connectivity is unavailable.
