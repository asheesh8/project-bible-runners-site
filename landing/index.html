Step 1Understand the initiative

A local library where the internet cannot reliably go.

VillageServer is a portable Raspberry Pi 5 system that creates its own Wi-Fi network. Nearby phones can open Bible text, audio Scripture, gospel films, children’s resources, and training without mobile data.
