The VillageServer Initiative

Spreading the gospel where internet cannot reach.

Mission statement

To carry Scripture, Christian media, and local-language discipleship resources into places with limited internet, unreliable electricity, and little access to Christian teaching.

Plain-language promise

A field team powers the kit. Nearby phones join its local Wi‑Fi. Families, churches, and schools can read, watch, listen, save, and share without using mobile data.

MissionPlain-language purpose

Technology that carries the gospel farther.

Many communities have limited internet access, unreliable electricity, few local-language resources, or little access to Christian teaching. VillageServer is built so a field team can carry a whole digital library into those places.

A nearby person does not need a data plan or technical background. They join the local VillageServer Wi‑Fi, open the library, and can read, listen, watch, save, and share gospel resources from their own phone.

How it worksSimple field model

Power in. Gospel library out.

VillageServer is meant to feel simple for the person using it: join Wi‑Fi, open the library, save what helps, then share it with someone nearby.
