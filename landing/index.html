The VillageServer Initiative

A whole village's Bible library. No internet required.

Where a paper Bible reaches one set of hands, a VillageServer reaches a village. It's a solar-powered Raspberry Pi that creates its own local WiFi — so anyone nearby can open Scripture, audio Bibles, and video in their own language on the phone already in their pocket. Completely offline.

☀️

1 · Solar power

A folding solar suitcase keeps the whole kit running through the day — no grid, no generator.

🍓

2 · Raspberry Pi server

A palm-sized computer holds Bibles, audio, and video pre-loaded in local languages.

📶

3 · Local WiFi hotspot

A travel router broadcasts a private network that covers the gathering — no data plan needed.

📱

4 · Every phone connects

People join the hotspot and read, listen, and watch — then share phone-to-phone to neighbors.
