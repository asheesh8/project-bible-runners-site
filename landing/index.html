MissionWhy it exists

Gospel access for places with limited internet, power, and teaching resources.

VillageServer is a portable local library for Scripture, Christian media, discipleship training, children’s resources, and practical ministry files.

It is built for missionaries and local leaders who need the gospel to keep moving even when the internet does not.
