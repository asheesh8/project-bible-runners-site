Phones, SD, and USB Sharing

Resources can move by local Wi‑Fi, phone-to-phone transfer, microSD card, USB drive, or computer-to-phone copy.

Plain answer

The goal is not to teach technology terms. The goal is to help one saved resource reach the next person.

Wi‑Fi library

Best for browsing and choosing resources while near the VillageServer kit.

microSD

Useful for language packs, Android phones, and carrying large files physically.

USB drive

Useful for computers, projectors, and prepared file handoffs.

Phone transfer

Best for one person sharing a saved resource directly with another nearby person.
