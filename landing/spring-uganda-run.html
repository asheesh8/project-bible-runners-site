What is the Spring Uganda Run?

A team. A load of Bibles. One mission.

Every spring, a team of Project Bible Runners volunteers travels into northern Uganda — one of the most Bible-scarce regions in East Africa. They carry hundreds of printed Bibles, solar-powered VillageServer kits, and the good news that followers of Jesus across the world see them and want them to have God's Word.

Many believers in these communities have waited years — some over a decade — for a Bible in their own language. On this run, we aim to place 800 Bibles and deploy two VillageServer libraries, giving entire villages offline access to Scripture for the first time.

Every dollar you give before the team departs goes directly to Bibles and equipment. No overhead. The team covers their own travel.

Campaign updates

From the field.

May 2026

Shipment confirmed — Bibles en route

800 Lugbara-language Bibles have been printed and are now in transit to our staging point in Gulu. The team departs in 18 days.

April 2026

Two VillageServer kits assembled

Both VillageServer kits for this run are built and loaded with the full YouVersion Bible library in 12 languages. Each one will serve a village of ~60 families.

March 2026

Team commissioned

Seven volunteers were commissioned and sent by their local churches to carry Bibles this spring. They are in active preparation. Please pray for them.
