Transfer Resources Between Devices

A simple transfer plan helps one saved resource move from one phone, computer, USB drive, or microSD card to another device.

Plain answer

Do not teach every method at once. Choose the route in front of you: iPhone to iPhone, Android to Android, iPhone to Android, computer to phone, or card to phone.

iPhone to iPhone

Use AirDrop first. It is usually the easiest Apple-to-Apple option.

Android to Android

Use Quick Share first. LocalSend is a strong backup when installed on both phones.

iPhone and Android

Use LocalSend, USB through a computer, or another shared storage route.

Computer to phone

Use a USB cable, Finder on Mac for iPhone, Apple Devices on Windows, or Android File Transfer style tools when needed.
