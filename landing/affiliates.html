Board brief - trusted partners

Ministry Resource Partners

Partners help with relationships, Scripture resources, digital libraries, nonprofit tools, field testing, and ministry credibility.

Plain answer

VillageServer should not pretend to do everything alone. The work is stronger when trusted partners provide content, relationships, hardware support, or field feedback.

Field steps

Use it this way

  1. Name the needDecide whether the gap is content, hardware, field access, training, nonprofit support, or translation.
  2. Match the partnerUse partners such as Project Bible Runners, Digital Bible Society, and TechSoup where they fit.
  3. Request clearlyAsk for the specific resource, license permission, discount, or relationship support needed.
  4. Credit properlyKeep partner names and content permissions visible and accurate.
  5. Review annuallyUpdate partner information as the initiative grows.
