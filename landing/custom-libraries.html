Content libraries & custom ministry resources

Your ministry. Your library.

While Digital Bible Society provides an extraordinary foundation of biblical resources in more than 1,700 languages, the VillageServer is not limited to those materials alone. Each organization can build a customized library that reflects its own calling and purpose.

A platform, not a fixed collection

Tailor the system to your mission.

Each church, ministry, missionary organization, Bible school, training center, or outreach program can create its own customized library by adding content to the VillageServer.

This allows every organization to tailor the system to its own ministry objectives, language requirements, teaching priorities, and outreach strategies. Content can be loaded onto memory cards or storage devices and added directly to the system.

Keep growing

A customized ministry resource center.

As new materials become available, they can be added to the system and distributed throughout churches, villages, schools, ministry centers, and outreach locations.

This flexibility allows every VillageServer to become a customized ministry resource center designed specifically for the people it serves — while still benefiting from the biblical foundation provided through Digital Bible Society and other approved content partners.
