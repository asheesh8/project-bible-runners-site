Testimonials

This page is for short field stories, comments, and board-friendly proof that the idea makes sense in real ministry contexts.

Plain answer

The strongest testimony is simple: people can receive, understand, keep, and share gospel resources even when a normal website cannot be reached.

What to collect

Short quotes from field workers, local leaders, trainers, and recipients.

What to avoid

Do not overstate numbers. Keep testimony honest, dated, and tied to real use.

Best story format

Before: no reliable access. During: connect locally. After: resources saved and shared.

Board takeaway

Evidence should be understandable in one minute and verifiable later.
