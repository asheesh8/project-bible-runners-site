Printable Pamphlets and Field FAQ

Printable PDFs help missionaries teach the same simple transfer and setup steps without needing to explain the whole website.

Plain answer

Handouts should be short enough to use in the field: one route, one page, one clear set of steps.

Field steps

Use it this way

  1. Pick the right handoutUse the PDF for the specific question: mission overview, phone transfer, power, setup, satellite, or FAQ.
  2. Download before travelSave PDFs on the phone, laptop, USB drive, and microSD card before leaving reliable internet.
  3. Print only what is neededKeep field packets light so people actually use them.
  4. Translate when possibleIf a local language version is needed, keep the same structure and simpler words.
  5. Store with the kitKeep printed handouts in the field case and digital copies in the library.
