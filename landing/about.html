About us

Ordinary people, joined by faith, serving through practical action.

We are a fellowship of Christian men from diverse professional, technical, ministry, and business backgrounds who have joined together to serve the Lord through practical action.

Who we are

Different skills, one purpose.

Our team includes individuals with experience in construction, technology, communications, education, project management, ministry outreach, logistics, and community development.

We believe God often calls ordinary people with different skills to work together toward extraordinary purposes.

What unites us

Not our backgrounds, but our faith.

What unites us is not our occupations or backgrounds, but our shared faith in Jesus Christ and our desire to use the gifts God has entrusted to us in service to others.

Our commitments

The principles we work by.

01

Integrity & stewardship

We are committed to integrity and faithful stewardship of the gifts, resources, and trust placed in our care.

02

Servant leadership

We seek to lead by serving, putting the needs of communities, churches, and local leaders first.

03

Biblical truth

We hold to biblical truth, and we work to help communities gain access to resources that strengthen families, churches, and local leaders.

Who we serve

Working alongside those reaching the unreached.

As opportunities arise, we work alongside missionaries, pastors, churches, ministries, and community leaders who share a desire to see lives transformed through the power of God's Word.

  • MissionariesCarrying Scripture and teaching into the field.
  • Pastors & churchesStrengthening local discipleship and leadership.
  • MinistriesExpanding outreach with practical tools.
  • Community leadersHelping families and communities access resources.
