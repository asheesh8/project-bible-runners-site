Board brief - who we are

About Us

VillageServer is a practical ministry initiative focused on offline access, simple training, and durable field tools.

Plain answer

The initiative exists to support people already doing ministry work. The technology should serve the mission, not become the center of the story.

Field steps

Use it this way

  1. Listen to partnersBegin with what missionaries and local leaders say they need, not what looks impressive.
  2. Use familiar toolsFavor phones, memory cards, simple Wi-Fi, printed steps, and repeatable habits.
  3. Package clearlyKeep every kit labeled, documented, and easy to explain in one conversation.
  4. Test with real usersRun the setup with people who are not technical and watch where they get stuck.
  5. Improve quietlyFix confusing steps, simplify language, and update the PDFs before broader rollout.

Posture

Humble, practical, field-tested, and partner-led.

Audience

Missionaries, board members, churches, local trainers, and ministry partners.

Promise

Make resource access simpler without pretending every field setting is the same.

Boundary

The initiative does not replace local ministry; it equips it.
