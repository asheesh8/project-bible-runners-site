The VillageServer Initiative

Spreading the gospel where internet cannot reach.

Mission statement

To carry Scripture, Christian media, and local-language discipleship resources into places with limited internet, unreliable electricity, and little access to Christian teaching.

Simple promise

Power the kit. Join its local Wi‑Fi. Open the library. Save and share Scripture, gospel media, training, and children’s resources without using mobile data.

MissionWhy it exists

Gospel access for places with limited internet, power, and teaching resources.

VillageServer is a portable local library for Scripture, Christian media, discipleship training, children’s resources, and practical ministry files.

It is built for missionaries and local leaders who need the gospel to keep moving even when the internet does not.
