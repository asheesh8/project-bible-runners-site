Satellite Systems and Content Acquisition

Satellite can support updates and communication, but the local library should remain useful even when satellite is off.

Plain answer

Satellite is optional. It is helpful for updates and remote coordination, but it should not become the foundation. VillageServer is strongest when the local library works offline first.

What satellite adds

A live connection for updates, support, messaging, and content refresh when the field team needs it.

What stays local

Scripture, films, training, and saved resources should still open from the local server without internet.

What to watch

Cost, power draw, line of sight, weather, data limits, and local regulations.

Board takeaway

Treat satellite as an upgrade path, not as the only way the ministry works.
