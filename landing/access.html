Where downloaded files go.

Android

Open Files or My Files. Look in Downloads, Internal storage, SD card, or the folder you selected while copying.

iPhone or iPad

Open Files, tap Browse, then look under On My iPhone or On My iPad. Connected readers appear under Locations.

Before leaving
