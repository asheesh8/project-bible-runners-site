Get and Share Resources

This page explains the normal user flow: connect to VillageServer Wi‑Fi, open the library, save a resource, and share it with someone nearby.

Plain answer

The user experience should feel like joining Wi‑Fi at a home or school. They should not need to know what a server is.

Step 1

Open Wi‑Fi settings and join the VillageServer network shown on the kit label.

Step 2

Open the local library address or follow the prompt shown by the phone.

Step 3

Choose one useful resource and save it for offline use.

Step 4

Share that saved resource using AirDrop, Quick Share, LocalSend, USB, or microSD.
