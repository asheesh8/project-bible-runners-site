A practical blueprint

Proven tools, not just possibilities.

This website serves as a practical resource center and blueprint for ministry. Rather than simply discussing possibilities, we focus on identifying proven tools, technologies, systems, and methods that can be applied in real-world ministry environments.

We seek to gather, organize, evaluate, and share information that enables Christian workers to accomplish their mission more effectively, especially in areas where traditional resources may be limited or unavailable.

What we provide

Seven ways we equip the field.

01

Technology research & evaluation

Information on available technologies, equipment, software, communications systems, and digital tools that support ministry outreach and biblical education.

02

Satellite & communications

Guidance on satellite communications, broadcast reception, digital content distribution, and delivering Christian resources into remote or underserved areas.

03

Village-based information systems

Practical guidance on portable, locally accessible digital libraries, educational systems, Scripture resources, and offline content delivery platforms.

04

Digital Bible & resource distribution

Methods for providing Bibles, teaching materials, discipleship resources, children's content, and leadership training through online and offline technology.

05

Missionary & church support

Information that helps pastors, missionaries, and ministry workers identify solutions that fit their unique field situations and objectives.

06

Training & implementation

Step-by-step guidance to help ministries understand, deploy, operate, and maintain technology solutions effectively and sustainably.

07

Best practices & field-tested solutions

Examples, case studies, lessons learned, and recommendations gathered from real-world ministry experiences and outreach efforts.

Our goal

Knowledge becomes most valuable when it is shared.

Our goal is to create a growing knowledge base that enables ministries to learn from one another, avoid unnecessary obstacles, and accelerate the spread of the Gospel through wise and effective use of technology.

By providing practical information, training resources, and proven strategies, we hope to help Christian workers around the world fulfill their calling more effectively.

Our blueprint

Connecting knowledge, technology, and ministry for the advancement of the Gospel.
