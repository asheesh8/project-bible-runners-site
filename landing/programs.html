Board brief - what we offer

Programs and Services

VillageServer services are organized around access paths: phones, local Wi-Fi libraries, power, projector teaching, satellite updates, and training materials.

Plain answer

The program is not one product. It is a set of practical access paths that can be combined based on the field need.

Field steps

Use it this way

  1. Assess the settingDocument language, device types, power, internet, and whether use is individual or group-based.
  2. Pick the access pathChoose phone transfer, microSD, USB, Raspberry Pi library, projector, power, or satellite.
  3. Prepare the kitLoad content, label cables, charge batteries, and pack the matching printed guides.
  4. Train the local userTeach the person who will repeat the setup after the visiting team leaves.
  5. Support and updateUse feedback and partner reports to improve content, equipment, and training.
