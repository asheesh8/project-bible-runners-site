Programs & Services

VillageServer organizes several field services into one simple directory: local libraries, phone sharing, power, projection, and optional satellite support.

Plain answer

A board member should not need to understand all the hardware. The program can be understood as four services: prepare content, power the kit, help phones connect, and train local users.

Local library kits

Raspberry Pi based libraries that nearby phones can open over local Wi‑Fi.

Phone distribution

microSD, USB, LocalSend, AirDrop, and Quick Share paths for moving files device to device.

Group media

Projector and audio options for showing gospel films, training, and Scripture media to groups.

Field support

Clear setup guides, printable handouts, and a board-ready directory for ministry partners.
